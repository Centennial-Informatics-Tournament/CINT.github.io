About

  • CInT, or Centennial Informatics Tournament, gives Middle and High School Students the opportunity to improve, learn, and apply programming skills with 20 algorithm based questions and various tasks or "challenges" related to programming.

    Not only will winners be eligible for prizes from our amazing sponsors, but will also be given opportunities to attend workshops held by students and guest speakers.



    Teams

    • Groups of up to four are allowed, but each student must register individually
    • Teams will be split into a High School division and Middle School division
    • All students will receive the same problems, but winners from each divison will be awarded prizes
    • Even one high schooler on a team will automatically place that team in the High School Division
