What is CINT?

HocoHacks is Howard County's high school hackathon, where students from various middle and high schools across the county will get the opportunity to col col-teamlaborate on coding projects and compete for prizes. Whether you want to learn to code, or to flex your coding abilities, HocoHacks brings together coders of all different skill sets to col col-teamlectively promote the knowledge of code.
