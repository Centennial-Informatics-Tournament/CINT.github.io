About

  • CInT, or Centennial Informatics Tournament, gives Middle and High School Students the opportunity to improve, learn, and apply programming/problem solving skills with 15 algorithm based questions and various tasks or "challenges" related to programming. This year, CInT will take place at Centennial High School (4300 Centennial Ln, Ellicott City, MD 21042)

    Not only will winners be eligible for prizes from our sponsors, but will also be given opportunities to attend workshops held by guest speakers.



    Teams

    • Groups of up to four are allowed, but each student must register individually.
    • Teams will be split into a Standard Division and an Advanced Division
    • Students are allowed to choose which division to register for, but greater prizes will be awarded for winners in the Advanced Division.
    • Prizes will be awarded to individual members on winning teams
